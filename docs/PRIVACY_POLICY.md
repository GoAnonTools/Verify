GoAnon Verify does not sell personal data, does not upload ID documents, does not transmit passphrases, and does not send browsing activity to GoAnon.

Credentials are stored locally in encrypted form. Proof history, when enabled, is stored locally on the user’s device.

In strong privacy mode, no government, issuer, wallet backend, or a GoAnon Verify server or any GoAnon ecosystem server is contacted during normal website proof use.
